## Overview

WiGig (802.11ad) dongle streaming over the 60GHz band. With a modified driver, line-of-sight latency is low enough for comfortable play.

## Setup

Requires a patched dongle driver and an unobstructed path between headset and dongle. Latency degrades fast when the signal is occluded.
